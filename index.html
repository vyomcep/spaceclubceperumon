About Us

Founded on April 7, 2023, Vyom Space Club is the premier student-led aerospace and astronomy organization at the College of Engineering Perumon.

Our club serves as a collaborative platform for students across all engineering disciplines to explore the complexities of space science. By integrating technical workshops, research initiatives, and observational sessions, Vyom aims to cultivate a culture of aerospace innovation within the CEP campus.

We are committed to empowering our members with the skills required to contribute to the future of space exploration.
